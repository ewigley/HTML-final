Session Information

Please see below for more information about sessions

Senior Photos
- Clients may bring up to 3 different outfits to change into.
- There will only be 1 location for the photo shoot, however there will be multiple places at the location.
- The client will receive 30 professionally edited photos.
- The senior photo shoot will last for 60 mins.
Family Photos
- Unless otherwise discussed with me, please do not bring a change of outfits for family photos
- There will only be 1 location for the photo shoot, however there will be multiple places at the location.
- The client will receive 15 professionally edited photos
- Family Photoshoots will last for 30 mins.
Maternity
- Clients may bring upto 5 different outfits to change into
- Client may choose upto 2 differnet locations.
- Client will recieve 20 professionally edited photos.
- Maternity photos shoots will last for 1.5 hours. Drive time is not included.
Weddings
- For wedding photos I can offer a full inclusive package or an "À la carte" style package.
- I have ability to plan all times and locations of any photos being taken for the wedding party and will communicate plans to the Bride and Groom for input.
- I have the ability to assist clients with coordinating with other wedding planners on the itinerary for photos.
- I have the ability to assist with planning other areas of the wedding day as needed
- Clients will recieve all of the photos taken during the agreed upon hours of work and will be professionally edited.
- Contracted work hours for the event range from 4 hours to 10 hours. See pricing below for more infomation
Corporate Events
- Clients using my services for corporate events should some of the following:
 -Will showcase the business in a positive manner
 -Will showcase employees in a positive manner.
 -Photographer has final say on what is acceptable to photograph and may choose to stop taking photographs
  -Should this happen photographer will pro-rate the rest of the event and still edit and give client photos that had already been taken
 -Further information will be included in the contract presented to the representative of the business
Freelance
